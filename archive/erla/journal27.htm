wally again. erinn, my parents, and myself were eating breakfast on the deck this morning when erinn spied the little guy down by the lake shore. he disappeared behind some bushes just before i went upstairs in the lodge to retrieve my camera. from upstairs i saw some cabin owners down by the lake fishing with a small boy. i rushed out to warn them and as i turned the corner of the lodge there he was, just above the people, who had not yet noticed his presence, and about fifteen feet in front of me. i yelled at him, which worked, thankfully, but i had no time to take a shot with my camera. my father was more successful with his shot, an effective bean-bag bullet which sent wally running.

the bottom photo here may look like a cloud, but it is actually the dust from a giant slab of the mountain side breaking off and crashing below.
