08.11.05

mike and steve left today after four days of intense activity here. they brought exactly 96 bottles of beer with them for the four days...turns out it was just a bit short. we are all of us out of control.

mike and i made it to lake at the falls, which is about 4 miles past rimrock lake. it was a good 20 mile hike, after which we were quite fatigued. i would feel more impressed with myself, except for the fact that we were passed by two ultra-marathon runners who actually went further than we did with a desparingly speedy gait. mike's 10 year old dog made it to the final lake, but his old legs gave out on the tough terrain on the way back, and he was carried a good three miles on mike's back.

the following are photos from the week...a week of activity and madness. by the way, the lightning fried my modem, so updates may not come as frequently as i would like.
