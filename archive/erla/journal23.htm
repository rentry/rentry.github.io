07.28.05

i wish i had photos from last night, but, alas, the camera cannot go everywhere.

dylan, and bozeman, were fantastic. i haven't been able to spend much time in bozeman, for several years anyway, and was pleased to return. bob put on his cowboy hat for "summer days" and lit up the arena. his tonal range may be dead, but it is still magic when he picks up the harmonica (or, if i may be so bold, the mouth-harp). for those that are interested, the set list is at: http://bobdylan.com/live/spring2005setlists.html#20050727

when we got back to the east rosebud valley at 1:00am we caught a glimps of the northern lights, great white spires just over the hill, a perfect ending to a great day.
