09.13.05

the snow is back and that means it 's time to leave here. we had the 'winter picnic' today down the valley with paul and helen nees hosting. we are desperately attempting to find a way to portland, as we have a lot of stuff and very little money.

this is the last update for this journal this summer. thank you to all who have kept up with our journey in this beautiful place. i hope to begin some kind of portland journal, if we ever get there.
