6.27.05

not too much to report as of now. erinn and i went up to the lily springs drainage and further up the mountain to the creek (sylvan) in order to divert water down to the cabins. it is my favorite work up here. before the fire in 1996, most of the cabins here relied on the spring and creek for drinking water, plumbing, and everything else, but most have dug wells by now. i enjoy the idea of low impact usage of resources, especially when they originate several hundred yards up the mountain.

we have had some exciting storms and interesting cloud formations dropping through the valley. last week's 80 degree weather has given over to 50's, but the moisture is a welcome change.

more soon--rj
